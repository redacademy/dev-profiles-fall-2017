Hometown

I was born in East Vancouver, just a few blocks east of commercial drive. When I was 10, my family moved to Coquitlam, and I went to High School in Port Coquitlam. If you don't know Port Coquitlam, a fun fact is that Canada's worst serial killer is from there!



Pre RED?

For the past 6 years I had been pursuing a career in music. I received my diploma in Contemporary and Jazz guitar from VCC in 2013, and I spent a year working as a lead guitarist for Carnival Cruise Lines where I completed 2 runs of the Western Caribbean. For the past year and a half I have been playing guitar professionally in Vancouver and teaching guitar both privately and as a contracted teacher for several different music schools. Something I didn't realize was that pursuing music as a source of income would have me almost exclusively playing cover music and background music-two things I don't love. I've also realized I don't love teaching guitar. And then one day at a family dinner I hear that my brother has enrolled in a digital marketing course at a school called RED Academy...
