A brief page about who I am, and what im all about!

Where I'm from

I grew up in Ottawa. I have lived in beautiful Pemberton for the past year. Prior to that I lived in Calgary. I am a huge lover of outdoor fun. I am passionate about a bunch of activities, so take a look below for some fun pics of what I like to get up to in my free time.

Why I'm here

I was a professional chef for the last few years. I have had the opportunity to travel to many interesting places persuing this career. After a ton of reflection, I came to the realization that I would be much happier following another life-long passion: computers! I have dabbled in coding before, but have never though of it as a career path.

One interesting fact about me: I am a coach on Pembertons (and soon Vancouvers) Gracie Jiu Jitsu team. I instruct kids and adults on the gentle art of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu. My coolest travel experience was spending a year in the British Virgin Islands as a private chef. My secret talent is cooking shawarma, a favorite meal of Ottawa ex-pats. After I graduate, I hope to one day work remotely in exotic locales all over the world.
